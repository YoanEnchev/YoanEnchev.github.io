Unlike most of the owls this one copes with extreeme enviroment. Snowy owls are native to the arctic, have thick fur and noble apperance. The magnificent look and ability to survive in extreemly low temperatures makes them Ð¼ajestic yet adorable.

Polar Bear

Massive, strong, with killing instincts, polar bears are tough predators in a harsh environment. They are at the top of the food chain and their diet is completely meat based. Frightful and terrifying yet tender and loving for their sweet precious cubs.

Arctic Fox

Being a tiny animal in an enviroment with extremly low temperatures, lack of food and tough predators isn't an easy job. Small and clever, Arctic fox manages to resist the harash enviroment and continue to be interesting to explore and adorable.

Penguin

Penguins are classified as birds, but they have adjusted to swimming rather than flying. They are excelent swimmers which helps them catch their pray. It's common knowledge that penguins inhabit Antarctica but that's not true about some species.
